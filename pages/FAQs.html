Answers to Common Questions About Our Export Services

Got Questions?

We Have Answers

Find answers to common questions about our export services, logistics, shipping, product quality and delivery.

What products do you export?
We export a wide range of high-quality agricultural products including fresh fruits, vegetables, spices, herbs, dry fruits, pulses, grains, flours, and dehydrated products — all meeting international quality standards.
Which countries do you export to?
We export to multiple countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, USA, Canada, Singapore, Australia, UK, Germany, and other European and African markets.
Do you provide international shipping?
Yes, we provide reliable international shipping via sea freight, air freight, and road transport with efficient logistics partners and real-time tracking support.
How do you ensure product quality?
All products go through strict multi-stage quality checks in our dedicated testing facility. We follow international food safety and export standards before every shipment.
What packaging methods do you use?
We use export-safe, tamper-proof, and hygienic packaging solutions including vacuum packs, moisture-proof bags, and carton boxes designed for long-distance transportation.
How can I place an order?
You can contact our team through the Contact Us page, or send your requirements directly via email at info@midiexports.com or call us during business hours.
Do you support bulk orders?
Yes, we handle both small and large bulk export orders. Our streamlined supply chain management ensures efficient processing for all order sizes.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery timelines depend on the destination country and the shipment method selected. We provide estimated delivery schedules at the time of order confirmation.
Are your products certified?
Yes, our products follow international export standards and certifications wherever required, including FSSAI and other food safety certifications.
Why choose MiDi Exports?
We offer certified quality products, competitive pricing, reliable logistics, complete documentation support, and dedicated customer service — making us a trusted partner for global buyers.
